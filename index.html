Welcome to Casa Victoria El Roque

Casa Victoria El Roque is an elegant private vacation villa located in the charming village of El Roque, Fuerteventura. This beautiful property offers three spacious bedrooms and three bathrooms, perfect for families or groups seeking comfort and privacy.

The villa features a heated outdoor pool open year-round, a terrace with outdoor seating area, and private parking. With free WiFi, air conditioning throughout, and a fully equipped modern kitchen, you have everything needed for an unforgettable vacation in the Canary Islands.

Located just 2.2 km from Playa del Castillo beach and 42 km from Fuerteventura Airport, Casa Victoria combines the tranquility of a private home with easy access to the island's most beautiful locations.

Location

El Roque, Fuerteventura

Casa Victoria El Roque is located in the charming village of El Roque on the beautiful island of Fuerteventura. The island is renowned for its endless beaches, crystal-clear waters, and year-round pleasant weather - perfect for sun lovers and water sports enthusiasts.

Distances to attractions:

  • Playa del Castillo Beach - 2.2 km
  • Eco Museo de Alcogida - 27 km
